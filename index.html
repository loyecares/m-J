How it all began

Every great love has a story worth telling.

The Beginning

The First Meeting

Life has a funny way of bringing people together at just the right moment. Joshua and Morenike crossed paths in a way neither could have predicted — and the world quietly shifted. There was something unmistakable in the air that day.

Getting to Know You

Slow Conversations & Deep Connections

What started as casual conversations became late-night calls, shared laughter, and the comfortable silence that only exists between people who truly understand each other. Joshua found in Morenike a brilliance that matched her warmth; Morenike found in Joshua a depth she had always hoped for.

The Realization

When Friendship Becomes Love

There was no single moment — it was a thousand small ones. The way he remembered every detail she shared. The way she made even ordinary days feel extraordinary. Before either could find the words, they already knew.

The Question

He Asked, She Said Yes

With a heart full of love and certainty, Joshua asked the question. Morenike's answer — immediate, joyful, and unwavering — set the stage for everything that follows. This wedding is the next page in a story that has only just begun.

Now & Always

Forever, Together

And now, surrounded by everyone they love, Joshua and Morenike step into the next chapter. Not as two separate stories, but as one. We are grateful you are here to turn this page with us.
